Announcements

Technical Assistance
If you experience issues, please contact GovOS Support by Live Chat or Email at GSSupport@GovOS.com

Tax Lien Registry
Effective November 1, 2019. The South Carolina Department of Revenue will implement a statewide lien registry for filing and indexing state tax liens and satisfactions. TAKE NOTE: Records of SC tax liens found on our site will not be updated after November 1st.

Virtual Viewer
We now offer an alternative image viewer that does not require Java. While Internet Explorer remains the preferred browser, browsers that do not have Java ( ex. Chrome and Windows Edge ) will use this alternative virtual image viewer. Please click on the link for additional information about the new viewer and some of its limitations: VIRTUAL IMAGE VIEWER

"Unverified" Watermark on Images
The “unverified” watermark indicates that the document is proceeding through the indexing and verification process in our system. The watermark will disappear once the verifying process is complete.

Google Chrome
We are excited to announce that Google Chrome can now be utilized to view images on our site!

Electronic Stamp on Document Images
Effective as of 10/06/15 the electronic stamp indicating the instrument number and the book and page on the document will move from being on the top, left hand corner of the image to the bottom right hand corner of the document image.

Documents Prior to 1990
Please note – while our documents are subcategorized for your convenience, due to the nature of the documents recorded prior to 1990 we recommend searching by book type and not relying on the subcategories.
