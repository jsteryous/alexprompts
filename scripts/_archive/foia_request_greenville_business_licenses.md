# FOIA Request — City of Greenville Business License Database

**Send to:** businesslicense@greenvillesc.gov  
**CC:** cityattorney@greenvillesc.gov (optional — signals you know the process)  
**Subject:** Freedom of Information Act Request — Business License Database Export

---

Dear City of Greenville Business License & Revenue Center,

Pursuant to the South Carolina Freedom of Information Act (S.C. Code Ann. § 30-4-10 et seq.), I am requesting a copy of the City of Greenville's current business license database in electronic format.

**Specifically, I am requesting the following fields for all active and recently renewed licenses:**

- Business name
- Owner name / responsible party name
- Business address (street, city, zip)
- License classification / business type code
- License number
- License status (active, renewed, voided)
- License issue date and most recent renewal date

**Preferred format:** CSV or Excel (.xlsx). If the system cannot export in either format, a pipe-delimited or tab-delimited flat file is acceptable.

I do not require social security numbers, personal residential addresses unrelated to the business, or any information your office considers exempt under § 30-4-40.

If any portion of this request is denied, please provide a written explanation identifying the specific statutory exemption relied upon for each withheld field, so that I may evaluate whether to appeal or narrow the request.

Per § 30-4-30, I understand the City has fifteen (15) business days to respond. If you anticipate the response will require additional time, please notify me in writing.

There is no commercial purpose to this request. If fees are required, please provide an estimate before proceeding so I may authorize them.
