No filler, every sentence carries information. Emit PLAIN standard markdown only; do NOT backslash-escape characters: write # and [COLD OPEN], never the escaped forms. Banned: in an unprecedented move, sent ripples, the AI landscape, game-changer, a new era, the rise of, it is worth noting, raises important questions, navigate the complexities, buckle up, dive into, did you know. FLOW RULE: do not dump facts and then opinions. Move the way a sharp mind moves. HEADLINE: a concrete, specific, real sentence, not a label. Gold standard: "Washington Just Reached Into a Live AI Product and Switched It Off."
